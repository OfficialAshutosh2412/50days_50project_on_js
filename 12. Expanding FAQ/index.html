frequently asked questions

What type of pen is it?

Customers often want to know if it's a ballpoint pen, rollerball pen, fountain pen, or another type

Does it come with ink refills?

People may inquire about whether the pen includes additional ink cartridges or if they need to purchase refills separately.

Is the pen refillable?

Customers might want to know if they can easily replace the ink in the pen once it runs out.

What is the pen made of?

Some customers may be interested in the materials used to manufacture the pen, especially if they have specific preferences or requirements.

Is the pen suitable for left-handed users?

Left-handed individuals may ask if the pen is designed to accommodate their writing style without smudging or discomfort.

Does it have a comfortable grip?

Potential buyers might inquire about the ergonomic features of the pen, particularly if they anticipate using it for extended periods.
